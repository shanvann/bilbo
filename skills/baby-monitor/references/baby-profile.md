# Baby Profile

## Feeding

- Fed on demand, every 0.5–4 hours (whenever hungry)
- Baby is removed from bassinet for feeding
- Expect ~30-60 min out of bassinet per feed (feed, burp, change, resettle)
- Out-of-bassinet gaps = feeding, changing, being held, or sleeping elsewhere (stroller, arms, etc.)
- Camera only monitors the bassinet — sleep outside it is not captured
