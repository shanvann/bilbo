# Baby Profile

## Feeding Schedule

- Every 3 hours: 11 PM, 2 AM, 5 AM, 8 AM, 11 AM, 2 PM, 5 PM, 8 PM
- Baby is removed from bassinet for feeding
- Baby typically needs to be woken up to feed — parents undress her (keep diaper on) to wake her by making her feel cold

## Feeding Window

- Expect ~30-60 min out of bassinet per feed (undress, wake, feed, burp, resettle)
- Out-of-bassinet gaps within ±45 min of a scheduled feed time should be labeled as "Feeding"
