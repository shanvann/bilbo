# Park Slope Parents (PSP) Classifieds Format

PSP Classifieds runs on Groups.io. Posts are email-style messages sent to the group.

## Platform

- URL: https://groups.parkslopeparents.com/g/Classifieds/topics
- Membership required to post
- Posts appear as threaded topics with subject line + body

## Post Structure

### Subject Line

Format: `[GIVING AWAY] Item Name — Neighborhood`

Examples:
- `[GIVING AWAY] Uppababy Vista Stroller — Park Slope`
- `[GIVING AWAY] Baby Clothes 0-6 months — Park Slope`
- `[GIVING AWAY] Crib + Mattress — Park Slope`

### Body

1. **Opening line**: Brief friendly intro (1 sentence)
2. **Item description**: What it is, brand/model if relevant, condition, age
3. **Photo reference**: Note that photos are attached (Groups.io supports image attachments)
4. **Pickup/contact**: How to coordinate
5. **Sign-off**: Friendly close

### Tone

- Friendly, casual, parent-to-parent
- Concise — no walls of text
- Honest about condition (scratches, wear, etc.)

### Example Post

Subject: `[GIVING AWAY] Halo Bassinest Swivel Sleeper — Park Slope`

Body:
```
Hi all,

Giving away our Halo Bassinest Swivel Sleeper. Used for about 6 months, in great condition — just some minor wear on the mesh sides. Clean and ready to go.

Photos attached.

If interested, please email me and we can coordinate pickup from my home.

Thanks!
```
